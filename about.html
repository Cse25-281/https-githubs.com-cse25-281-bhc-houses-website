About Botswana Housing Corporation

Botswana Housing Corporation provides affordable, modern and quality housing solutions across Botswana.

Our mission is to help citizens find safe, comfortable and beautiful home

The Botswana Housing Corporation (BHC) is a government-owned organization responsible for the development and management of residential properties across Botswana.

BHC Houses are built with the aim of providing affordable, safe, and quality housing for citizens. These homes are located in various towns and cities, helping to reduce housing shortages in the country.

With a focus on modern design and long-term durability, BHC ensures that each housing project supports comfortable living and community development.

Through its projects, BHC plays an important role in improving access to housing and supporting national development goals.
